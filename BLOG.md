# Automate Your ServiceTitan Workflows with Our New n8n Community Node

We're excited to announce that Velocity BPA has just released a new community node for n8n that brings powerful ServiceTitan integration to your automation workflows!

## The Challenge

Field service businesses running on ServiceTitan often find themselves juggling multiple tools and manually transferring data between systems. Whether it's syncing customer information to your CRM, triggering notifications when jobs are completed, or coordinating dispatch schedules with external calendars, these repetitive tasks consume valuable time that could be better spent growing your business.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-servicetitan

Our new ServiceTitan node bridges the gap between your field service operations and the rest of your business ecosystem. Built specifically for n8n, this node enables you to automate workflows involving customers, jobs, appointments, and dispatch operations—all without writing a single line of code.

## Key Features

The n8n-nodes-servicetitan node provides seamless access to core ServiceTitan functionality:

- **Customer Management**: Automatically sync customer data, create new customer records, and update existing information across your tech stack
- **Job Operations**: Trigger workflows based on job status changes, create jobs from form submissions, and keep your team informed in real-time
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Coordinate appointments with your calendar systems, send automated reminders, and manage scheduling conflicts
- **Dispatch Coordination**: Streamline your dispatch operations by integrating with communication tools, routing systems, and resource management platforms

## Getting Started

Installing the ServiceTitan node is quick and easy. Simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-servicetitan


Once installed, the ServiceTitan node will appear in your n8n node palette, ready to be dragged into your workflows.

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically creating Slack notifications when high-priority jobs are dispatched, syncing completed job data to your accounting software, or triggering follow-up email sequences based on appointment completions. These are just a few examples of what becomes possible when you connect ServiceTitan to n8n's ecosystem of 400+ integrations.
